Executive Summary

The OPA Firewall Policy Automation platform reduces manual intervention from 24 to 3 humans per firewall rule, yielding £1,010 cost savings per rule and freeing up 42 hours of skilled staff time per rule processed. At baseline processing of 100 rules annually, this represents £100,960 in annual operational savings and enables redeployment of 21 FTEs to higher-value activities.
